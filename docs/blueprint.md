# **App Name**: Mamaearth Growth Garden

## Core Features:

- Daily Login Reward: Award Leaf Points for daily app visits, encouraging habitual engagement.
- Routine Completion Reward: Grant Leaf Points for completing morning and evening skincare routines, fostering product usage.
- Streak Bonus: Automatically award a bonus for maintaining a 7-day streak of completed skincare routines.
- Skin Quiz Reward: Provide Leaf Points for completing a skin quiz, gathering user data and providing personalized recommendations.
- Referral Bonus: Incentivize user referrals with Leaf Points for each successful friend invitation.
- Feedback Reward: Award points for providing feedback on purchased products, enhancing product development and customer satisfaction.
- Spin-the-Leaf Wheel: Provide a wheel-spinning mini-game that allows users to win bonus points or discounts if they have completed a routine.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft green (#90EE90) to evoke natural wellness and align with Mamaearth's brand.
- Background color: Very light desaturated green (#F0FFF0) to provide a clean, soothing backdrop.
- Accent color: Pale yellow (#FAFAD2) to add a touch of warmth and highlight key elements.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that combines a modern look and a little warmth or personality; suitable for headlines or body text
- Use simple, rounded icons in a light green color to represent actions, rewards, and progress.
- Employ a mobile-responsive, single-page layout with rounded cards to maintain a user-friendly experience.
- Incorporate subtle animations for reward notifications and interactive elements such as the 'Spin-the-Leaf' wheel to enhance user engagement.
